This site is a home for technical writing, project notes, and reflections on the work that sits between curiosity and execution.

The goal is simple:

- write clearly about data science and software
- share projects in a way that feels useful, not performative
- keep the publishing workflow lightweight enough that writing stays easy

Over time, this space will collect practical lessons, project breakdowns, and notes on what is actually worth paying attention to.
